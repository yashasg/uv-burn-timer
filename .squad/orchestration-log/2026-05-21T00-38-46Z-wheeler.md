### Critical Science: Cloud Cover Dominance

UV transmission is **exponentially sensitive** to cloud optical properties. Beyond day 3, cloud-cover forecast error dominates the UVI error budget, not ozone or aerosol error. This is established science (Bais et al., ECMWF technical docs) and the line where WHO/WMO stop publishing operational UVI products. **The data layer is not the limit. The science is.**

### Data Availability (WeatherKit)

- `WeatherService.dailyForecast()` → up to **10 days** `DayWeather` with daily-max `uvIndex`.
- `WeatherService.hourlyForecast()` → hourly weather including `uvIndex` over comparable window.
- Practical horizon: D+1 through D+10 daily UVI available; science defensibility drops past day 5.
