⚠️ Important Notice
Estimated burn time only. Not medical advice. Skin response varies. This app is for informational purposes only — it is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. The burn-time estimate is a model calculation, not a measurement, and assumes consistent conditions. It is not a substitute for sunscreen reapplication, shade, protective clothing, or a dermatologist's guidance. When in doubt, cover up or reapply sunscreen. For children, consult a pediatrician for sun-safety guidance.
About

Burn-time calculation based on the Fitzpatrick skin phototype scale (Fitzpatrick 1988) and the Diffey erythemal action spectrum (Diffey 1991; CIE Standard S 007/E-1998). Not affiliated with any dermatology organization.
